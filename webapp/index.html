What to Do If You Think You Have Skin Cancer.

If you suspect you have skin cancer, act promptly. Schedule an appointment with a healthcare professional for a thorough evaluation. Monitor any changes in your skin, such as new growths or changes in existing moles. Early detection is crucial, so seek medical advice as soon as possible for accurate diagnosis and timely treatment.
